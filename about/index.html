About My Work

I am a passionate game developer. Currently I am working for Lightspeed & Quantum Studio in Tencent Games as a game engine developer.

I took part in development and optimization of PUBGM, Cross Fire Mobile， Call of Duty Mobile and Apex Legends Mobile.

Before joining Tencent, I took part in development of Nikki 4th and Hidden Dragon: Shadow Trace.

I mostly focus on Unreal Engine 4 and Unity development. And I am skilled in physics&animation development and optimization.

I am also skilled in work-flow development. I can develop in Maya and Motionbuilder, both in python and c++. On the other hand, I love to play with Houdini and I am really interested in PCG stuff.
